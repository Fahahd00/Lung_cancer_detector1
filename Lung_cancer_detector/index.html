What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence is a modern technology that enables computers to simulate human mental capabilities and learn from data. In this system, we use advanced algorithms to analyze X-ray images and recognize patterns and potential spots, which helps in early and accurate diagnosis of lung diseases.

How the System Works

This system processes X-ray images by extracting advanced features related to color intensity distribution, dark spots, and edges. It then uses an advanced machine learning model (Gradient Boosting) to classify the image and determine whether the lung is healthy or needs specialized medical examination.
